Our PHS SkillsUSA club has had some very bright students and teams achieve great things in competitions and during their time in the organization. These are some of the most notable ones.

President's Volunteer Service Award

This year Amruta S. was awarded the President's Volunteer Service Award - Gold Level through SkillsUSA. This award is presented to individuals who have served a certain number of hours over a 12-month period or cumulative hours earned over their lifetime. Recipients can recieve an official lapel pin, a personalized certificate, and a letter from the president of the United States for their achivement.

State Finishers

Last year, our 2 Additive Manufacturing teams both advanced to state and finished within Top 10. Ashley L. and Braden L. placed 6th and Sudar S. and Puranjay P. placed 5th. Since this was our organization's first year, such a showing at the state level was a very important accomplishment for our teams.
