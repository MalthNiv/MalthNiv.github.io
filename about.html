Who are we?

Just as our slogan states, the PHS SkillsUSA club is passionate about our futures and we commit ourselves to our goals with the utmost sense of determination.














For the 2022 - 2023, the PHS SkillsUSA club had grown in members significantly from last year, when we only had 4 members. This year we had over 30 members signed up for competitions at district in all different industries. Unfortunately, due to severe weather conditions during the weekend of districts, our chapter was not able to compete.

Instead, members who were not able to compete at districts were given the opportunity to create Job Exhibits and advance to state in that way. We had several people advance including our two Additive Manufacturing teams, 2 members in Medical Assisting, and Job Exhibits in Web Page Design, 2D/3D Animation, Engineering Related Design, and Health Science Display.
